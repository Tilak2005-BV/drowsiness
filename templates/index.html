How it works

Powered by vision AI

Six signal streams processed entirely on-device. Your camera feed never leaves your machine.

01
👁

Eye Aspect Ratio

EAR computed from 6 landmark points per eye. Stays below 0.25 for 20+ consecutive frames → drowsiness alert fires instantly.

02
😮

Yawn Detection

Mouth Aspect Ratio tracks jaw opening in real-time. MAR over 0.65 signals fatigue — your earliest warning before full drowsiness.

03
📐

Head Pose

3D head orientation via solvePnP across 468 landmarks. Forward head nodding — the microsleep signature — triggers alert.

04
⚡

Instant Alerts

Audio alarm + full-screen flash within 0.7 seconds of drowsiness onset. Designed to be physically impossible to sleep through.

05

100% Private

All processing runs locally via MediaPipe. Your webcam feed is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

06
